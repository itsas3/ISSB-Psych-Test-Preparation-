# ISSB Psych Test Preparation Tool

This is a web-based training tool designed to help candidates prepare for the Inter-Services Selection Board (ISSB) psychological assessment. The platform features timed modules to practice the **Word Association Test (WAT)** and **Sentence Completion Test (SCT)** in both English and Urdu.

## Features

*   **Timed Practice:** Each prompt is displayed for exactly 10 seconds, simulating the actual ISSB testing environment.
*   **Dual-Language Support:** Includes separate practice modules for English and Urdu.
*   **Dynamic Learning:** Words and sentences are randomized on every test start to ensure you do not memorize the order.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** A clean, minimalist design to reduce distractions and help you focus on your performance.
*   **Mobile-Friendly:** Designed to be used on your phone or laptop while on the go.

## Modules

The tool includes the following test modules:
1.  **WAT English:** Rapid word association in English.
2.  **WAT Urdu:** Rapid word association in Urdu.
3.  **SC English:** Sentence completion prompts to practice quick, positive, and logical writing.
4.  **SC Urdu:** Sentence completion prompts in Urdu.

## How to Use

1.  **Preparation:** Keep a pen and paper numbered 1–100 ready before you start a session.
2.  **Selection:** Choose your desired test type from the main menu.
3.  **Action:** When the test starts, write down your response as soon as the word or sentence prompt appears.
4.  **Discipline:** If you do not finish a sentence within the 10-second timer, leave it and move immediately to the next prompt to maintain the test's flow.

## Guidelines for Success

*   **Be Authentic:** Interviewers are looking for your natural personality, not rehearsed answers.
*   **Stay Positive:** Focus on constructive, realistic, and problem-solving responses for all prompts.
*   **Avoid Cliches:** Try to provide fresh, personal thoughts rather than overused proverbs.
*   **Consistency:** Practice regularly to improve your writing speed and clarity of thought under pressure.
